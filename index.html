Clearer Skies Coaching

Chronic illness, whether physical or mental, is a challenging battle. You may be feeling grief, loss, failure, and isolation, or you may be struggling with a perpetuating mental illness as a result. While this battle may often seem invisible to your friends and family, it's something you deal with every moment of every day. My goal as a coach isn't to lead you to a life of rainbows and clear skies. Part of my role is to acknowledge that bad days may come, but so will good days. Together, our goal is to improve your life with clearer days.
